Why Green?

The Problem

"Due to poor waste management systems around the world, the majority of plastic packaging that is sent to landfills ends up in our natural environments. 8 million tons of plastic is dumped in the ocean every year. Plastic pollution threatens wildlife, alters ecosystems and poses risks to human health. Reducing the production of plastic packaging would decrease the chance of such damage."

Plastic packaging is a massive environmental problem. On average, Amazon sells over $410 million worth of products every single day and considering that Amazon utilizes plastic in the majority of their packaging, their shipping and delivery practices are not sustainable. When you shop using Amazon or Ebay you are contributing to the problem.

The Solution

Together Green is an E-commerce store that aims to provide more sustainable means of delivery and packaging.

We aim to tackle climate change and ocean pollution by using sustainable packaging such as switching from plastic air bubbles and bubble wrap, which have been proven to clutter our oceans, to biodegradable and recycled packaging.

Together Green targets customers who want to reduce their carbon footprint and who want to make more environmentally conscious decisions.

Afterall, Together Green is tomorrow's clean!
